# DisposAI - Complete Advertising & Marketing Guide
## Every Feature, Screen & Element for Promotional Content

---

## 🎯 **APP OVERVIEW**
**DisposAI** is an AI-powered medicine disposal app that helps users safely dispose of expired or unused medications while protecting the environment. It combines cutting-edge AI technology with gamification to make eco-friendly disposal engaging and rewarding.
